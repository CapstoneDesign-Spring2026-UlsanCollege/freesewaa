
# Free Sewaa — Project Idea Pitch

> A community-centered platform for sharing reusable items through donation, promoting sustainability, reducing waste, and supporting people in need.

---

## Overview

**Free Sewaa** is a web-based community donation platform designed to connect people who want to give away usable items with those who need them. The project focuses on creating a simple, practical, and accessible system that encourages the reuse of functional goods instead of letting them go to waste.

Many items such as clothes, books, furniture, electronics, and household goods are often discarded even though they are still useful. At the same time, many individuals and families struggle to afford essential items. Free Sewaa aims to bridge this gap by providing a digital platform where communities can share resources more effectively.

This project is being developed as part of a **Capstone Design Project** and is intended to demonstrate both technical implementation and meaningful social impact.

## 1. Project Title

**Free Sewaa — Community Donation and Resource Sharing Platform**

---

## 2. Problem

Many useful items such as clothes, books, furniture, and electronics are often thrown away even though they remain in usable condition. At the same time, many people struggle to afford basic necessities or lack access to free reusable goods.

People who want to donate items often do not know where to share them, while people in need may not know where to find available resources. This creates unnecessary waste, reduces opportunities for community support, and limits the effective reuse of valuable items.

Free Sewaa aims to solve this problem by providing a simple and accessible platform that connects donors with people who need reusable goods.

---

## 3. Target Users

The main users of this system include:

- Students
- Low-income individuals and families
- Community members who want to donate usable items
- Anyone looking for free reusable goods

The platform is intended to help local communities share resources in a practical, organized, and meaningful way.

---

## 4. What the System Will Do

Free Sewaa will provide a web-based platform where users can post items they want to donate and others can browse available items.

Users will be able to:

- Post items for donation
- View available donated items
- Read item details
- Request an item
- Contact the donor to arrange pickup or delivery

The system will simplify the donation process, encourage resource reuse, and help reduce unnecessary waste.

---

## 5. Core Features (First Version)

The first version of the system will include the following features:

- Post an item for donation
- View available donated items
- Request an item
- Contact the donor
- Display item information such as category, description, and location

These features represent the core workflow of the platform.

---

## 6. Demo Scenario

The final demonstration will show a simple end-to-end interaction with the system.

**Step 1**  
A user opens the Free Sewaa website.

**Step 2**  
A donor posts a donation item, such as a used textbook.

**Step 3**  
Another user browses the list of available items.

**Step 4**  
The user selects the item, submits a request, and contacts the donor.

This demo will illustrate how the platform connects donors with recipients through a clear and practical workflow.

---

## 7. Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The MVP will be a basic but functional web application that allows users to:

- Post donation items
- View available items
- Request an item
- Contact the donor

This version will demonstrate the core idea of the project: connecting people who want to donate useful items with those who need them.

The initial focus will be on delivering a reliable and demo-ready system before expanding to additional features.
