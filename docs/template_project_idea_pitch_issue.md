## 1. Project Title

**Free Sewaa — Community Donation and Resource Sharing Platform**

---

## 2. Project Summary

**Free Sewaa** is a web-based platform designed to connect people who want to donate usable items with individuals who need them. The system focuses on making donation simple, organized, and accessible for local communities.

The project promotes **resource reuse**, **waste reduction**, and **community support** by creating a digital space where functional items can be shared instead of discarded.

Our goal is to build a practical and meaningful platform that demonstrates how technology can support both **social good** and **environmental sustainability**.

---

## 3. Problem

Many useful items such as clothes, books, furniture, and electronics are often thrown away even though they are still in usable condition. At the same time, many people struggle to afford basic necessities or do not know where to find free reusable items.

People who want to donate items often lack a clear and convenient way to reach those who need them. Likewise, individuals in need may not know where to look for available donations in their community.

This gap creates several problems:

- Usable resources are wasted
- People in need miss access to helpful items
- Communities lose opportunities for mutual support
- Unnecessary disposal increases environmental impact

**Free Sewaa** aims to solve this problem by providing a simple digital platform that helps donors and recipients connect directly.

---

## 4. Target Users

The primary users of this platform include:

- Students
- Low-income individuals and families
- Community members who want to donate usable items
- People looking for free reusable goods

The platform is especially valuable for users who need affordable access to everyday items and for donors who want a simple way to make their unused belongings helpful to others.

---

## 5. What the System Will Do

Free Sewaa will provide a web-based platform where users can post items they want to donate and others can browse those available items.

The system will allow users to:

- Add donation listings with basic item details
- Browse and view available items
- Request an item they are interested in
- Contact the donor to arrange pickup or delivery
- View item information such as category, description, and location

By supporting this workflow, the platform will make community donation easier, more organized, and more accessible.

---

## 6. Core Features (First Version)

The first version of the platform will focus on the essential features needed to demonstrate the main donation process.

### Core Features
- Post an item for donation
- View available donated items
- Request an item
- Contact the donor
- Display item details such as:
  - Title
  - Category
  - Description
  - Location

These features represent the foundation of the project and will form the basis of the **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)**.

---

## 7. Demo Scenario

The final demonstration will present a clear end-to-end workflow showing how the platform operates.

### Demo Flow

**Step 1**  
A user opens the Free Sewaa website.

**Step 2**  
A donor posts a donation item, such as a used textbook.

**Step 3**  
Another user browses the list of available donated items.

**Step 4**  
The user selects the item and reads its details.

**Step 5**  
The user submits a request and contacts the donor to arrange pickup.

This demo will show how the system successfully connects donors with recipients in a simple and practical way.

---

## 8. Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The MVP will be a basic but functional web application that demonstrates the core idea of the project.

### MVP Goals
The MVP will allow users to:

- Post donation items
- View a list of available items
- Request an item
- Contact the donor

The first version will focus on **reliability**, **clarity**, and **demo readiness** rather than advanced functionality. The priority is to ensure that the main workflow works smoothly from start to finish.

## 10. Risks or Unknowns

Some possible challenges include:

- Limited experience with backend development
- Learning GitHub collaboration and version control workflows
- Integrating frontend and backend components correctly
- Designing a simple and effective database structure
- Managing time effectively to complete the MVP within the semester

These risks will be reduced by building the project step by step, reviewing progress weekly, and testing each feature as it is developed.

---

## 11. Scope Check

This project is:

- **Demoable**
- **Feasible**
- **Well-scoped**

The team will begin with a small, functional MVP and only add additional features after the core workflow is complete and stable.

This approach will help ensure that the final system is realistic, testable, and ready for demonstration.

---

## 12. Expected Impact

Free Sewaa is intended to create positive value in both social and environmental ways.

### Social Impact
- Helps people access useful items at no cost
- Encourages community support and sharing
- Makes donation more convenient and accessible

### Environmental Impact
- Reduces waste of usable goods
- Encourages reuse instead of disposal
- Supports more sustainable community practices

---

## 13. Future Improvements

After the MVP is completed, the platform may be extended with additional features such as:

- User registration and login
- Search functionality
- Category filters
- Notification system for requests
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- Image upload for donated items
- Request history or donation status tracking

These improvements would increase usability and make the platform more practical for long-term use.

---

## 14. Conclusion

Free Sewaa is a meaningful and practical capstone project that addresses a real community problem. By creating a simple donation and resource-sharing platform, the project aims to reduce waste, promote reuse, and support people who need essential items.

The project is intentionally scoped to begin with a realistic MVP, allowing the team to focus on building a working system that clearly demonstrates its value.
