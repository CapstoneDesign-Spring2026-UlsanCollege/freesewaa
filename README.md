# Free Sewaa

> A community donation and resource-sharing platform that connects people who want to give away usable items with those who need them.

Free Sewaa is a community-focused platform where people can donate items for free to others in need. The project is designed to reduce waste, encourage reuse, and support individuals who may not be able to afford essential goods.

By making the donation process simple and accessible, Free Sewaa helps build stronger communities while promoting sustainability and responsible resource sharing.

---

## Project Overview

Many useful items such as clothes, books, furniture, and electronics are often discarded even though they remain in good condition. At the same time, many individuals and families struggle to afford basic necessities.

Free Sewaa addresses this problem by providing a simple digital platform where users can post items for donation, browse available items, request what they need, and contact donors to arrange pickup.

This project is being developed as part of a **Capstone Design Project** and demonstrates the practical use of web technologies to solve a real community problem.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of Free Sewaa is to create a platform that:

- reduces waste by encouraging the reuse of functional items
- supports people who need essential goods
- makes community donation easier and more organized
- promotes social good through technology

The system is intended to provide a simple and meaningful solution for connecting donors and recipients within a local community.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people have usable items they no longer need, but they often do not know where to donate them. On the other hand, people who need these items may not know where to find free and reusable resources.

This creates several problems:

- usable goods are wasted
- people in need lose access to helpful resources
- communities miss opportunities for support and sharing
- unnecessary disposal increases environmental impact

Free Sewaa aims to bridge this gap through a user-friendly donation platform.

---

## Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The first working version of Free Sewaa will include the following core features:

1. Users can post an item for donation  
2. Users can view a list of available items  
3. Users can request an item  
4. Users can contact the donor  

The MVP focuses on delivering the main donation workflow in a clear, functional, and demo-ready form.

---

## Core Features

- Post donation items with basic details
- Browse available donated items
- View item information
- Request an item
- Contact the donor for pickup or coordination

### Item Details May Include

- Title
- Description
- Category
- Location
- Contact information

---

## Target Users

Free Sewaa is designed for:

- students
- low-income individuals and families
- community members who want to donate usable items
- anyone looking for free reusable goods

---

## Demo Scenario

The final demonstration will show a complete user flow:

1. A user opens the Free Sewaa website  
2. A donor posts a donation item such as a used textbook  
3. Another user browses the available items  
4. The user selects the item and sends a request  
5. The user contacts the donor to arrange pickup  

This workflow demonstrates how the platform connects donors with recipients in a practical and meaningful way.
